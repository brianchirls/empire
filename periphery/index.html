Peripery

On the outer edges of the colonial domain, the borders between fact and fiction break down. In the US, a man discovers the reality behind his childhood fantasies.

In Australia, a community searches for certainty in a sea of dreams.

Crafted from two stories filmed on opposite sides of the world, Empire: Periphery allows you to experience truth and fiction side by side.

Schiphol Airport handles 1100 flights on average per day.

Of those flights, 6 to 7 planes are carrying the body of a deceased person. A great number of these deceased people were born in former Dutch colonies, and are now returning to the countries of their birth for final burial.
